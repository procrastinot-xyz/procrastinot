Why three Versions?

The free version is unsurprisingly an incentive for new users to check out our platform. Not only do they get free access to all of the learning resources, they can also already get started on reducing next months payment. It is a bit gutted in terms of features and functions as well as flexibility, we don't want anyone to try and stay on the free version forever.

The home version is our standard offer. For just €20 you will have access to our grade A studying material - anytime, anywhere. Through completing set tasks in various courses you will be able to reduce the next month's payment. If you work hard enough, it will even be free! Have kids? Our optional control tools allow you to check your child's progress and effort - and maybe they will even earn some extra pocket money.

The business version is an offer for our corporate clients. In today's fast moving world, employers need to stay on top of new developments; and so does their workforce. We provide an offer to get 100 licenses for only €399 and our control tools allow monitoring individual employee's progress.

Apart from a few select features, every version is functionally the same. Fast, responsive and ad-free. We do not want our users to ever have a negative experience on our platform and still have enough deterrent to disincentivize using the free version indefinitely.
